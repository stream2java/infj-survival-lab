I'm Max — on a working holiday in Australia, somewhere between lost and looking.

For most of my life, I gave more than I needed to, to people who couldn't hold it. I didn't know I was allowed to just exist.

This is where I document the experiments — small actions taken in the fog, with a north star to keep me from walking in circles.
